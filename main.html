Features These are the features of our product

Face ID

Face ID is such a useful features as it allows you to unlock your iPhone X through simply looking at it. Face ID is more secure than our previous security mechanism, Touch ID. The possibility of someone breaking into your phone has been lowered. Now you don't have to worry about a crazy girlfriend unlocking your phone while you are asleep. They would need to have you keep your eyes open and looking at the phone. You are welcome, fellas.

Control Center

Control Center has now been taken away from the bottom of the screen to the top right. This is in order to allow people to close an app through the bottom of the screen, in absence of a home button. For those customers that have trouble reaching the top of the screen, we have kept reachability. To activiate it, simply swipe down in the bottom area of the iPhone. Then swipe down on the top right area to activate Control Center. From there, it works like how you are used to.

App Switcher

Due to the lack of a home button, we have created a new way to activate the App Switcher. Just like closing an app, it involves the bottom of the screen. Instead of fully swiping to close the app, hold the swipe and it will show the rest of the applications that you have opened or frozen. From here, you can swipe to the right see the rest of the applications, and click on the one desired. To close any of the applications that you have open, simply swipe up on the card until the card leaves the screen.
